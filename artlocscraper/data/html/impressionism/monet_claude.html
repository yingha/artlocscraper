Claude Monet

[French Impressionist Painter, 1840-1926]

• Also known as:  Claude Oscar Monet
• Relationships: Studied under Charles Gleyre.
Monet's students included American Impressionists Theodore Butler, Lilla Cabot Perry and Theodore Robinson.
